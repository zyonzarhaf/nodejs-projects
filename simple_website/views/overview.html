course overview

In this course, you will learn the fundamentals of Node.js and its role in server-side JavaScript development. You will master the core modules and APIs in Node.js and utilize NPM (Node Package Manager) to manage third-party libraries. You will build RESTful APIs using Node and Express.js, store and retrieve data using Mongoose, implement data validation, handle authentication and authorization, handle and log errors effectively, and write unit and integration tests.

The course is perfectly structured into a series of bite-sized, easy-to-follow videos that cover both theory and practice. You will have access to 15 hours of HD video, 220 lessons, downloadable content, hands-on exercises, English captions, a certificate of completion, and lifetime access. You can learn at your own pace and track your progress with the free "My Learning" program.
